## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No login, no credit card, no catch. You see your results instantly. We ask for your email after — never before.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: We verify every price against official vendor pricing pages weekly. The date of last verification is shown in our pricing sources file. If you find an error, email us.

**Q: What do you do with my data?**
A: Your tool spend data is used only to generate your audit. We store it to generate your shareable link. We never sell it. If you give us your email, Credex may reach out for high-savings cases — you can unsubscribe instantly.

**Q: Who is Credex?**
A: Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits — Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise, and others — sourced from companies that overforecast or pivoted. This tool is free because it helps us find people who are overspending and can benefit from what we offer.

**Q: What if my savings are $0?**
A: We'll tell you honestly. "You're spending well" is a valid and useful audit result. We don't manufacture fake savings — that would make the tool worthless.

## FAQ

### Is this free?
Yes. You can run an audit without creating an account or entering an email.

### How accurate are the savings?
Savings are based on publicly available vendor pricing and deterministic rules. The recommendations are designed to be defensible, not hype.

### Do you store my email or company name?
Only if you choose to capture the report by email. Shared links strip identifying details.

### Why do some tools recommend dropping one subscription entirely?
When two tools overlap heavily for the same workflow, keeping both often adds cost without much extra value.

### Will you spam me?
No. The capture flow says no spam, and unsubscribe handling is part of the backend flow.
